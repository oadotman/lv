## Work-Life Balance Strategies

### Setting Boundaries

**Physical Boundaries**:
- Separate work space
- Work computer only
- Office hours sign
- Work phone silenced after hours

**Mental Boundaries**:
- Morning transition ritual
- End-of-day shutdown
- Weekend email limits
- Vacation auto-responders

### Avoiding Burnout

**Warning Signs**:
- Working 14+ hour days
- Weekend work becoming normal
- Eating at desk daily
- No exercise routine
- Family complaints increasing

**Prevention Strategies**:
```
Daily: 30-minute lunch break
       15-minute walks
       Defined stop time

Weekly: One full day off
        Social interaction
        Exercise 3x

Monthly: Long weekend
         Review boundaries
         Evaluate productivity
```
