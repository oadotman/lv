# Complete Guide to Freight Broker License Requirements in 2026

**Getting your freight broker license is the first step to building a profitable brokerage.** This comprehensive guide walks you through every requirement, form, and fee needed to get your authority.

## Quick Overview: What You Need

### Essential Requirements
- **MC Number**: Motor Carrier number from FMCSA
- **DOT Number**: Department of Transportation registration
- **Surety Bond**: $75,000 freight broker bond
- **Process Agent**: BOC-3 filing in every state
- **Business Entity**: LLC or Corporation recommended

### Total Cost Breakdown
| Item | Cost | Timeline |
|------|------|----------|
| MC Number | $300 | 3-4 weeks |
| Surety Bond | $938-$9,000/year | 1-3 days |
| Process Agent | $40-$50 | Same day |
| Business Formation | $50-$500 | 1-7 days |
| **Total** | **$1,328-$9,850** | **4-5 weeks** |

## Step-by-Step Licensing Process

### Step 1: Form Your Business Entity
**Timeline**: 1-7 days
**Cost**: $50-$500

**Options**:
- **LLC** (Recommended): Personal asset protection
- **Corporation**: Better for multiple owners
- **Sole Proprietorship**: Simplest but risky

**How to Form an LLC**:
1. Choose business name
2. File Articles of Organization
3. Get EIN from IRS (free)
4. Open business bank account

### Step 2: Get Your DOT Number
**Timeline**: Immediate
**Cost**: Free

**Apply at**: www.fmcsa.dot.gov
**Required Information**:
- Business name and address
- EIN or SSN
- Number of vehicles (enter 0)
- Type of operation

### Step 3: Apply for MC Number
**Timeline**: 3-4 weeks
**Cost**: $300

**Application Process**:
1. Complete OP-1 form online
2. Select "Broker of Property"
3. Pay $300 fee
4. Wait for processing

**Required Information**:
- DOT number
- Business structure
- Insurance details
- Banking information

### Step 4: Obtain Freight Broker Bond
**Timeline**: 1-3 days
**Cost**: $938-$9,000/year

**Bond Costs by Credit Score**:
| Credit Score | Annual Premium |
|--------------|----------------|
| 700+ | $938-$1,500 |
| 650-699 | $1,500-$3,000 |
| 600-649 | $3,000-$4,500 |
| Below 600 | $4,500-$9,000 |

**Top Bond Providers**:
1. **Surety Bonds Direct**: Fast approval
2. **JW Surety Bonds**: Good rates
3. **Bryant Surety**: Bad credit options
4. **Alpha Surety**: Payment plans

### Step 5: Designate Process Agents
**Timeline**: Same day
**Cost**: $40-$50

**BOC-3 Filing**:
- Required in all 50 states
- Handles legal documents
- Annual renewal needed

**Recommended Services**:
- LegalZoom: $125
- FMCSA Filing: $40
- URS Agents: $45

### Step 6: Wait for Authority
**Timeline**: 10-20 business days
**Status**: Check daily at safer.fmcsa.dot.gov

**During Waiting Period**:
- Set up business systems
- Create carrier packets
- Build shipper relationships
- Get TMS software ready

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Wrong Business Classification
❌ Selecting "Carrier" instead of "Broker"
✅ Always choose "Broker of Property (except Household Goods)"

### 2. Insufficient Bond Coverage
❌ Getting less than $75,000 bond
✅ Exactly $75,000 required by law

### 3. Missing Process Agent Filing
❌ Only filing in home state
✅ BOC-3 covers all 50 states

### 4. Operating Before Active
❌ Booking loads during waiting period
✅ Wait for "Active" status

### 5. Using Personal Accounts
❌ Mixing personal and business funds
✅ Separate business bank account

## Insurance Requirements

### Mandatory Coverage
- **Surety Bond**: $75,000 (required)
- **General Liability**: Not required but recommended
- **Contingent Cargo**: Highly recommended

### Optional but Recommended
- **Errors & Omissions**: $1M coverage
- **Cyber Liability**: Data breach protection
- **Business Auto**: If using vehicles
