## Breaking Down Freight Broker Compensation

### Base Salary vs Commission

Most freight brokers earn through a combination of:
- **Base Salary**: 40-60% of total compensation
- **Commission**: 40-60% of total compensation
- **Bonuses**: 5-15% additional (performance-based)

### Commission Structures Explained

#### 1. Percentage of Gross Profit (Most Common)
- **Entry Level**: 25-35% of gross profit
- **Experienced**: 40-50% of gross profit
- **Top Performers**: 50-60% of gross profit + bonuses

**Example Calculation**:
- You book a load: Shipper pays $2,000, Carrier gets $1,700
- Gross profit: $300
- Your commission (40%): $120

#### 2. Flat Commission Per Load
- **Average**: $50-$150 per load
- **High-value/specialty**: $200-$500 per load
- **Best for**: High-volume brokers

#### 3. Tiered Commission Structure
- First $10,000 gross profit: 25% commission
- $10,001-$25,000: 35% commission
- $25,001-$50,000: 45% commission
- $50,000+: 50-60% commission
